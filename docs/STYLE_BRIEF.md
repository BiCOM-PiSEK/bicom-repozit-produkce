# Designový zákoník: STYLE BRIEF (Quiet Luxury)

> **ZÁVAZNÉ PRAVIDLO PRO VŠECHNY AGENTY A VÝVOJÁŘE:**
> Tento dokument definuje vizuální podpis, atmosféru a styl celého projektu Bicom Písek. Při tvorbě rozložení, stylů (Tailwind) a komponent musíte tyto definice **přísně dodržet**. Odchylky od této palety a přístupu jsou zakázány.

## Visual Style
The aesthetic bridges high-touch wellness with a refined minimalist corporate identity, explicitly utilizing a **quiet luxury** framework. It balances serene, high-end lifestyle photography with a professional technical blueprint style to communicate premium wellness and technological reliability.

## Composition
Layouts are strictly structured around a modular grid, utilizing generous negative space to separate complex technical diagrams from strategic messaging. Subjects in photography are centered for a sense of balance, while informational slides rely on a logical, horizontal flow to map out digital architectures.

## Attitude
The brand voice is deeply serene and meditative in its lifestyle presentation, yet transitions into a highly analytical and authoritative tone within its strategic documentation. It projects a sense of sophisticated security and data-driven empathy for its target demographic.

## Colors
The palette is anchored by a flagship combination of:
*   **Muted sage green** (např. `#738A75`)
*   **Warm cream tones** (např. `#FAF8F5`)
*   **Metallic gold** for high-signal iconography (např. `#C5A880`)
*   **Deep forest green** pro vysoký kontrast a dodání vizuální váhy/bezpečnosti technickým sekcím (např. `#3A4A3C`)

## Lighting
Lifestyle imagery is defined by diffused natural light that eliminates harsh shadows to create a breathable, airy atmosphere. Digital components incorporate a soft, luminous glow around key interface elements to suggest active processing and an ethereal quality to the technology.

## Camera
Photography utilizes a wide aspect ratio and an eye-level perspective to create an immersive, invitational environment. Strategic visuals are presented with a flat, graphic clarity, while lifestyle shots maintain a shallow depth of field to emphasize personal tranquility.
